Our story

We met on a rainy afternoon, both reaching for the same small table in a crowded café. We shared tea, then hours, then a slow sense of familiarity.

Some days felt like a soft page turning: shared walks, handwritten notes, and dinners that stretched into the night. In those quiet moments, our home grew.

We do not remember the exact day we decided, only the feeling of calm certainty. This celebration is a gentle thank-you for the love that brought us here.
